How to Download Videos

Follow these simple steps to download any video in your preferred quality.

1

Copy the Video URL

Go to the video you want to download (YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, etc.). Copy the URL from your browser's address bar.

Tip: On mobile, tap the Share button and select Copy Link.
2

Paste & Fetch

Paste the copied URL into the input field above and click the "Fetch Video" button. VidGrab will analyze the video and show all available formats.

Tip: You can also press Enter after pasting to fetch instantly.
3

Choose Your Quality

Browse the available formats — from 360p to 4K for video, or various bitrates for audio. Each card shows the format, file size, and codec info.

4K 1080p 720p 480p 360p MP3
4

Download & Enjoy

Click on any format card or the download button to start downloading. The file will save directly to your device's Downloads folder.

Tip: Want audio only? Click "Extract Best Quality MP3" for the highest quality audio file.

Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms are supported?

VidGrab supports 1000+ platforms including YouTube, Vimeo, Dailymotion, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Reddit, SoundCloud, and many more.

Is it free to use?

Yes! VidGrab is completely free with no hidden charges, no sign-up required, and no ads. Just paste and download.

Can I download audio only?

Absolutely! After fetching a video, you'll see audio format options below the video formats. You can also click "Extract Best Quality MP3" for the highest quality audio conversion.

Why is the download slow or failing?

Download speed depends on the source server. If a download fails, try a different quality or format. Some platforms may restrict downloads for certain content.

Is my data safe?

Yes. VidGrab doesn't store any of your data. We don't log URLs, don't use cookies for tracking, and don't collect personal information. Everything is processed in real-time and discarded.
